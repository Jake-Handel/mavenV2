## Design Premise
### For Laptops and Desktops
- Retro themed, similair to www.baroutro.com.au 
- Main home page will have a grayed out background image excluding the MAVEN logo and the coffee machine, taking the width of the screen (leave the image black for the moment). Then will have a semi-circle shape on the right hand side which will have the links to the pages, with hover effects making them larger and pop out.
- The Contact, menu, and directions pages will have a different coloured image as the background with their respective content centered on the page.
- The photos page will have a grid of images with a lightbox effect when clicked, really fashionable and presentable

### For Mobile and Tablets
- All images will be adjusted for the aspect ratio
- Instead of the semicircle, the links will be in a dropdown list on the right side of the screen
- Everything will be centered and responsive to work with phones
